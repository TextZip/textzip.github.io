Previously, I worked as a **Robotics Research Engineer** at [Field AI](https://fieldai.com/), where I developed learning-based policies for loco-manipulation, designed large-scale simulation infrastructures for unit testing and data generation, and explored risk-bounded foundational models to enhance robotic autonomy.

Before that, I was part of the [CNRS-AIST Joint Robotics Lab, IRL - Japan](https://unit.aist.go.jp/jrl-22022/index_en.html) as a **Technical Research Staff**, working under the supervision of [Dr.Mitsuharu Morisawa](https://unit.aist.go.jp/jrl-22022/en/members/member-morisawa.html). My research there focused on implementing Deep Reinforcement Learning (RL) policies for quadruped locomotion in unstructured environments and studying Sim2Real transfer for RL policies in both humanoids and quadrupeds.

In my free time, I enjoy working on hands-on projects that bring together electronics, programming, and design. I document most of my experiments and create open resources here to help and inspire others in the robotics community.
